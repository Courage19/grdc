About Us

GRDC, or Goromonzi Rural District Council, is dedicated to the allocation of stands in the Goromonzi area. Our mission is to facilitate sustainable development and ensure fair distribution of land resources.

Frequently Asked Questions

GRDC stands for Goromonzi Rural District Council, focusing on the allocation of stands in the Goromonzi area for sustainable development.

You can apply for a stand by filling out our online application form on the website or visiting our office for assistance.

Applicants must provide proof of residency and comply with local zoning regulations. Specific requirements can be found in the application guidelines.
